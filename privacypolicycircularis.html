Privacy Policy for Circularis - Elapse Watchfaces This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information in relation to your use of Circularis - Elapse Watchfaces (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. 1. Collection of Information The App may collect the following types of information for the sole purpose of displaying relevant data on your watchface: a. Health Data: The App may access and display data related to your heart rate. This data is only displayed on the watchface and is not transmitted or stored externally. b. Fitness Data: The App may access and display data related to the number of steps taken. This data is only displayed on the watchface and is not transmitted or stored externally. c. Notification Data: The App may access and display the number of unread notifications on your paired mobile device. This data is only displayed on the watchface and is not transmitted or stored externally. 2. Use of Information We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the data mentioned above. The data is solely used for the purpose of presenting it on the watchface, and no other use is made of this information. 3.
